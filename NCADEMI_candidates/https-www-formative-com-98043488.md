# Formative
**Vendor:** Formative
**Description:** Formative is an EdTech platform that empowers teachers to create and deliver interactive assignments, track student progress in real-time, and provide immediate feedback across diverse subjects and grade levels. The platform offers various question types and features designed to support differentiated instruction and student accommodations, aiming to accelerate learning and provide insights into student understanding. It supports individual teachers, schools, and districts in enhancing instruction and assessment practices.
**Product Website:** https://www.formative.com/

### AI Generated Insights
Formative states its commitment to fostering an accessible learning environment and continually improving its product to meet accessibility standards. The platform leverages Level Access for vetting site flows to identify accessibility issues and strives to conform with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 AA and Section 508 standards. Specific accessibility features include text-to-speech, closed-captioned video, audio question types, audio student response, and keyboard navigation. Teachers can implement various accommodations for students with special needs, such as text-to-speech, time limit extensions, and answer choice reduction, particularly for School & District license holders. While Formative emphasizes teacher guidelines for creating accessible assignments, no direct third-party accessibility audits or comprehensive reviews of Formative's accessibility posture were found in the provided research, though general discussions on EdTech accessibility and formative assessment tools are present.
