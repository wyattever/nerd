# Mathway
**Vendor:** Mathway, LLC
**Description:** Mathway is an online educational technology product that serves as a comprehensive math problem solver. It assists students, parents, and teachers by providing tools to understand and solve mathematical problems across various subjects, including pre-algebra, algebra, trigonometry, calculus, and statistics, offering instant answers and, with a premium subscription, step-by-step solutions. Users can input problems via typing, speaking, or by taking a picture.
