# NoRedInk
**Vendor:** NoRedInk
**Description:** NoRedInk is an online writing and grammar curriculum platform for grades 3-12 that uses adaptive exercises and personalized content to help students improve their writing skills. It provides teachers with tools for instruction, practice, and assessment, including data-driven insights into student growth and features for academic integrity.
