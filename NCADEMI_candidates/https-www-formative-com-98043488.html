Vendor: Formative

Formative is an EdTech platform that empowers teachers to create and deliver interactive assignments, track student progress in real-time, and provide immediate feedback across diverse subjects and grade levels. The platform offers various question types and features designed to support differentiated instruction and student accommodations, aiming to accelerate learning and provide insights into student understanding. It supports individual teachers, schools, and districts in enhancing instruction and assessment practices.

AI Generated Insights

Formative is committed to fostering an accessible learning environment and continually improving its product to meet accessibility standards, striving to conform with WCAG 2.2 AA and Section 508. The platform leverages Level Access for vetting site flows to identify accessibility issues. Key accessibility features include text-to-speech, closed-captioned video, audio question types, audio student response, and keyboard navigation. Teachers can implement various accommodations for students with special needs, such as text-to-speech, time limit extensions, and answer choice reduction, especially for School & District license holders. While the platform emphasizes teacher guidelines for creating accessible assignments, direct third-party accessibility audits or comprehensive reviews of its accessibility posture were not readily available, though a VPAT completed by Level Access exists. Formative is recognized for its commitment to accessibility through features like multimodal response options and its focus on supporting diverse learning needs.
