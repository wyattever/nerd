# DeltaMath
**Vendor:** DeltaMath
**Description:** DeltaMath is an online platform designed for math practice and assessment for middle and high school students. It provides interactive problems, immediate feedback, and covers a wide array of mathematical topics from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus. Teachers can create customizable assignments, quizzes, and tests, while students can track their progress and receive step-by-step solutions for incorrect answers.

### AI Generated Insights
DeltaMath aims to provide an accessible learning experience, stating conformance with WCAG 2.2 for its student application. The platform includes various accessibility features such as screen-readable content, alt text for static images, Braille printout options for complex diagrams, video captioning, and extensive keyboard functionality for interactive elements like graphing and reordering objects. While the majority of content is accessible, the vendor acknowledges that some interactive components may lack full screen reader or keyboard accessibility, and advises teachers to refer to a list of inaccessible content and suitable alternatives. Third-party sources also note DeltaMath's device compatibility and its emphasis on practice and mastery for various skill levels.

DeltaMath provides an explicit accessibility statement detailing its efforts to conform to WCAG 2.2 guidelines. This statement highlights that while a majority of content is fully accessible, some interactive elements are keyboard accessible but not screen reader friendly, and a few are not accessible by either method.
