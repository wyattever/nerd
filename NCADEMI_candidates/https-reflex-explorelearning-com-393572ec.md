# Reflex
**Vendor:** ExploreLearning
**Description:** Reflex is an evidence-based, adaptive, and individualized EdTech solution designed to help students in grades 2 and up master basic math facts in addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division through engaging, game-based activities. It aims to build math confidence and fluency by providing personalized coaching and real-time progress monitoring.

### AI Generated Insights
Reflex by ExploreLearning is an educational technology product focused on math fact fluency for grades 2 and up, aiming to be accessible to all students. The platform is highly visual with minimal text and offers keyboard controls to support navigation without a mouse or touch device. Closed captioning is available for voiced audio, and audio/music are optional. Reflex also measures keyboard entry speed to differentiate response times and features contrasting colors for many components. While it lacks adjustable font size or text zooming functionality, it offers personalized learning paths and is noted for supporting special education students and English language learners with features like Spanish and French language options and by factoring out keyboard speed from fluency calculations. The product strives to make learning engaging through game-based activities and rewards.
