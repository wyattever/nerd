# DreamBox
**Vendor:** DreamBox Learning
**Description:** DreamBox offers adaptive digital math and reading programs for students in grades K-8, designed to deliver personalized instruction within an engaging, gamified learning environment. The platform aims to build conceptual understanding, promote critical thinking, and support a variety of learning styles.

### AI Generated Insights
DreamBox Math, part of Discovery Education, has received significant accessibility enhancements in January 2025, primarily for K-5 students, aiming to exceed Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) standards. Key improvements include enhanced compatibility with assistive technologies such as screen readers and magnification tools, along with intuitive keyboard navigation and clear visuals to support students with low vision and color blindness. The program also features an improved screen reader with alternative descriptions to aid concept understanding. Closed captions are available across all K-8 Math lessons and academic videos in both English and Spanish to support diverse learners. While the Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) for K-5 math generally indicates support for WCAG criteria, it notes that approximately 5% of K-5 lessons currently do not meet all requirements, with ongoing efforts to address these areas.
