Vendor: CK-12 Foundation

CK-12 Foundation provides a comprehensive platform offering free online textbooks (FlexBooks), videos, adaptive practice, simulations, and real-world applications for K-12 students. The platform integrates AI to deliver personalized educational content and resources across various subjects, aiming to make quality education accessible.
