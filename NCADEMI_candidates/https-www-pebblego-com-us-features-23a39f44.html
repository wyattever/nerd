PebbleGo

Vendor: Capstone

PebbleGo is a digital content learning hub designed for K-3 students, offering interactive informational articles, read-aloud audio, and ready-made activities to support research, reading, and learning in core subject areas. It aims to provide equitable access for all learners, including those with disabilities, through various accessibility features.

AI Generated Insights

PebbleGo demonstrates a strong commitment to accessibility, aiming to comply with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA, Section 508, and EN 301 549 standards. The platform incorporates robust features such as text-to-speech, screen reader support, keyboard navigation, alternative text, video transcripts, and careful color contrast design. Its Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT), along with consistent commendation from third-party reviews and educational institutions, validates these efforts. These features, combined with intuitive navigation and leveled texts, provide multimodal supports that benefit a diverse range of K-5 learners, including those with disabilities and English language learners. Designed to be mobile-responsive and optimized for various learning environments, PebbleGo ensures an equitable and inclusive learning experience for all students.
