Vendor: Pear Deck Learning

Pear Deck is an interactive technology tool that transforms presentation slides into opportunities for formative assessment and active learning. It functions as an add-on to platforms like Google Slides, enabling teachers to create engaging lessons with various question types, provide real-time feedback, and differentiate instruction.

AI Generated Insights

Pear Deck demonstrates a commitment to accessibility, primarily through its integration with Microsoft's Immersive Reader, offering text-to-speech, translation, and adjustable text settings. The platform provides various accommodation features for assessments, including customizable text, high-contrast themes, and audio response options. A 2023 Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) claims conformance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA, though analysis indicates partial conformance for freehand drawing and annotation tools, which could affect users of some assistive technologies. The vendor emphasizes a rigorous, human-led testing approach in developing its VPATs, indicating a proactive stance on digital accessibility. Educational institutions and government bodies consistently highlight Pear Deck's utility in creating inclusive learning environments and supporting diverse learners through its features.
