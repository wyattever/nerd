# Book Creator
**Vendor:** Tools for Schools Limited
**Description:** Book Creator is an intuitive EdTech platform designed to empower students and teachers to create and publish interactive digital books incorporating text, images, audio, and video. It supports diverse learning styles and encourages creative expression, making learning accessible and engaging for all users.

### AI Generated Insights
Book Creator demonstrates a strong commitment to accessibility, incorporating features guided by Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles. The platform provides accessible authoring tools such as AI-generated alt text, speech-to-text in numerous languages, and options for customizable fonts and high-contrast colors. Compliance with WCAG 2.2 Level AA, EN 301 549, and Revised Section 508 is affirmed through their Accessibility Conformance Report, which was updated in March 2026. Proactive testing with various assistive technologies ensures broad compatibility. While the platform offers robust accessibility, the ultimate accessibility of individual books created within it depends on author adherence to accessible design practices.
