# SparkNotes
**Vendor:** Barnes & Noble Education
**Description:** SparkNotes provides comprehensive online study guides and educational resources for students across various subjects, including literature, history, and science. It offers summaries, critical analyses, and quizzes, alongside modern English translations of classic texts. The platform aims to help students understand complex schoolwork and prepare for tests.
