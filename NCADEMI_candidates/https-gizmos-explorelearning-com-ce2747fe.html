AI Generated Insights

Gizmos offers interactive STEM simulations for grades 3-12, incorporating several accessibility features. Many simulations aim for keyboard accessibility with simple keystrokes, though some interactive elements may lack full keyboard control. Content generally uses minimal text, supports browser zoom for resizing, and allows sound effects to be toggled. Most simulations are not time-dependent, and color contrast is largely maintained, although highly visual simulations might challenge users with severe visual impairments. While a public-facing VPAT was not found, institutional policies and reports indicate that educational bodies consider Gizmos' accessibility during procurement and integration into their digital learning environments. ExploreLearning is committed to ongoing accessibility enhancements for its products.
