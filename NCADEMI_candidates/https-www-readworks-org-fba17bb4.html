Vendor: ReadWorks, Inc.

ReadWorks is an EdTech nonprofit offering free, research-based resources like K-12 texts, instructional routines, and professional development to enhance reading comprehension. Its online platform provides a vast library of reading materials, digital tools, and assessment features to support teachers and students, with a particular focus on under-resourced communities.

AI Generated Insights

ReadWorks demonstrates an ongoing commitment to accessibility, striving to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, a conformance level also acknowledged by educational organizations. The platform undergoes both manual and automated testing across various browsers and with assistive technologies like VoiceOver and Lighthouse. While aiming for full operability with keyboard navigation and assistive technologies, some areas for improvement exist, including specific keyboard interaction limitations, color contrast issues, unlabeled form fields, and missing image text alternatives. These identified non-conformities are actively audited and addressed. Users can readily access support contact information to report accessibility issues.
