Vendor: CommonLit

CommonLit is a nonprofit education technology organization that provides a free digital library of high-quality reading passages and a comprehensive ELA curriculum for grades 3-12. It offers instructional materials, embedded assessments, and professional development to support teachers in enhancing students' reading, writing, and critical thinking skills.

AI Generated Insights

CommonLit demonstrates a strong commitment to digital accessibility, aiming for compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and the Americans with Disabilities Act. The platform integrates a suite of features designed to support diverse learners, including text-to-speech functionality, translation into over 30 languages, and digital annotation tools. It also offers a Guided Reading Mode, is optimized for mobile use, and is compatible with screen readers. These embedded accessibility tools are widely recognized and reviewed by various educational institutions and organizations. External evaluations often highlight their value for differentiated instruction and enhancing student engagement, particularly for students with disabilities and English language learners. Ongoing external scrutiny, including the use of accessibility testing tools, further indicates the platform's dedication to inclusive design and its role in broader digital equity discussions.
