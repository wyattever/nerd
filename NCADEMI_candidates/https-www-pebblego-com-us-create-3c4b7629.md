# PebbleGo Create
**Vendor:** Capstone
**Description:** PebbleGo Create is a digital workspace designed for K-5 students to demonstrate their understanding through engaging, age-appropriate creative projects. It allows students to add text, images, stickers, animations, and record audio, providing a versatile tool for formative assessments within the PebbleGo platform.
