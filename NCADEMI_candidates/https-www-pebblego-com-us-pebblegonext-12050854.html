PebbleGo Next

Vendor: Capstone

PebbleGo Next is a K–5+ digital learning platform that provides educators with resources to help students develop literacy skills and build knowledge in science and social studies. It offers developmentally appropriate content in English and Spanish, featuring read-aloud audio, rich vocabulary support, and multimodal learning tools. The platform aims to empower every student to read, learn, explore, and create independently.
