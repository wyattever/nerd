# CommonLit
**Vendor:** CommonLit
**Description:** CommonLit is a nonprofit education technology organization that provides a free digital library of high-quality reading passages and a comprehensive ELA curriculum for grades 3-12. It offers instructional materials, embedded assessments, and professional development to support teachers in enhancing students' reading, writing, and critical thinking skills.
**Product Website:** https://www.commonlit.org/

### AI Generated Insights
CommonLit demonstrates a commitment to digital accessibility, striving for compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and the Americans with Disabilities Act. The platform integrates several accessibility features to support diverse learners, including text-to-speech functionality, translation into over 30 languages, and digital annotation tools. It also offers a Guided Reading Mode to aid comprehension and is designed for mobile optimization and screen-reader compatibility. Vendor resources detail these efforts and the product's inclusive design principles. Third-party reviews often highlight these built-in tools as valuable for differentiated instruction and enhancing student engagement across various learning needs.
