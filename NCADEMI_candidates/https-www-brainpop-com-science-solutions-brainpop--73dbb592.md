# BrainPOP Science
**Vendor:** BrainPOP
**Description:** BrainPOP Science offers engaging, standards-aligned investigations focusing on real-world phenomena to foster scientific thinking. It integrates formative assessments, data manipulatives, simulations, and Claim-Evidence-Reasoning (CER) processes, helping students develop deep scientific knowledge and critical thinking skills.

### AI Generated Insights
Description: BrainPOP demonstrates a strong commitment to accessibility, actively incorporating Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles to support diverse student needs. The platform offers features like closed captioning, adjustable video speeds, audio descriptions, and integration with Microsoft Immersive Reader, catering to various learning styles and accessibility requirements. The company provides a Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) detailing conformance with WCAG standards, though it notes some aspects are "partially supported," such as certain dynamic content lacking pause options and occasional issues with keyboard navigation focus. BrainPOP regularly publishes resources and updates highlighting its accessibility enhancements and support for special education.

### AI Generated Insights
BrainPOP Science incorporates several accessibility features aimed at supporting diverse learners. The platform integrates with Microsoft Immersive Reader, providing tools like Line Focus, Text Personalization, Grammar Options, Read Aloud, Picture Dictionary, and translation into over 60 languages for various content types, including phenomena, simulations, and related readings. Audio descriptions are available for BrainPOP movies, offering added narration for visual details crucial for students with visual impairments. The platform also emphasizes accessible problem statements in its engineering projects to facilitate engagement for all students. Additionally, BrainPOP supports the use of graphic organizers and templates to break down information and offers differentiated learning paths to accommodate various learning styles and abilities.
