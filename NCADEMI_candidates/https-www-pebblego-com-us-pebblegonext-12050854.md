# PebbleGo Next
**Vendor:** Capstone
**Description:** PebbleGo Next is a K–5+ digital learning platform that provides educators with resources to help students develop literacy skills and build knowledge in science and social studies. It offers developmentally appropriate content in English and Spanish, featuring read-aloud audio, rich vocabulary support, and multimodal learning tools. The platform aims to empower every student to read, learn, explore, and create independently.

PebbleGo Next, developed by Capstone, is committed to accessibility, striving to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA, Section 508, and EN 301 549 standards. Capstone conducts regular accessibility testing and provides employee training to ensure continuous improvement in its digital products.
