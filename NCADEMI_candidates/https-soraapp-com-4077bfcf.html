Vendor: OverDrive

Sora is a free digital reading app developed by OverDrive for K-12 students. It provides access to ebooks, audiobooks, and magazines from participating school and public libraries, enabling students to read on their devices.

AI Generated Insights

Sora is designed with a strong commitment to accessibility, providing an inclusive digital reading experience for K-12 students. The platform adheres to WCAG 2.0 and 2.1 Level A and AA standards, with its conformance detailed in an Accessibility Conformance Report. It offers extensive screen reader support for popular tools like VoiceOver, NVDA, and JAWS, alongside keyboard navigation and accessible page structures. Key features include high contrast mode, dyslexic font, adjustable text sizes, and "Read to Me" text-to-speech functionality. Additionally, the "Content Bundles" feature empowers educators to provide differentiated access to digital books for specific student needs. These elements collectively demonstrate Sora's robust framework for supporting diverse learners within school library systems.
