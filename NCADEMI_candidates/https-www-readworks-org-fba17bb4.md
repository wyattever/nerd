# ReadWorks
**Vendor:** ReadWorks, Inc.
**Description:** ReadWorks is an EdTech nonprofit offering free, research-based resources like K-12 texts, instructional routines, and professional development to enhance reading comprehension. Its online platform provides a vast library of reading materials, digital tools, and assessment features to support teachers and students, with a particular focus on under-resourced communities.

### AI Generated Insights
ReadWorks maintains an ongoing commitment to accessibility, as detailed in its public accessibility statement. The platform strives to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, employing both manual and automated testing with various browsers and assistive technologies like VoiceOver and Lighthouse. While acknowledging some areas for improvement, such as keyboard navigation limitations, color contrast issues, unlabeled form fields, and missing image text alternatives, the vendor actively audits and addresses these non-conformities. Third-party reviews generally highlight ReadWorks as a free, web-based tool for reading comprehension, praised for its research-backed content, differentiation capabilities, and integration with platforms like Google Classroom. Support contact information is readily available for users to report accessibility issues.
