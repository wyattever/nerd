# BrainPOP ELL
**Vendor:** BrainPOP
**Description:** BrainPOP ELL is an interactive language learning platform that provides English language learners with engaging content, including animated movies, games, quizzes, and interactive features, to develop their listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills. The platform supports skill development through conversational English modeled by characters and integrates vocabulary, language, and content lessons.
**Product Website:** https://www.brainpop.com/classroom-solutions/products/brainpop-ell

### AI Generated Insights
BrainPOP ELL is designed with Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles to support diverse learners, particularly English language learners and students with special needs. The platform integrates features such as closed captions, audio descriptions for movies, adjustable playback speed, and full transcripts to enhance accessibility. It also utilizes Microsoft Immersive Reader for literacy support and offers multilingual movie options in Spanish and French. BrainPOP ELL provides various interactive activities that allow students to demonstrate understanding through different modalities, aligning with multiple means of representation, engagement, and expression. While the vendor provides extensive documentation on accessibility features, a third-party review, based on BrainPOP's VPAT, highlights areas of partial support regarding focus order and elements having discernible names, roles, and values, indicating some ongoing accessibility considerations.
