# PebbleGo
**Vendor:** Capstone
**Description:** PebbleGo is a digital content learning hub designed for K-3 students, offering interactive informational articles, read-aloud audio, and ready-made activities to support research, reading, and learning in core subject areas. It aims to provide equitable access for all learners, including those with disabilities, through various accessibility features.

### AI Generated Insights
PebbleGo demonstrates a strong commitment to accessibility, with its vendor Capstone actively working to comply with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA, Section 508, and EN 301 549. Official documentation highlights robust features such as text-to-speech, video transcripts, alternative text for images, screen reader support, keyboard navigation, and careful color contrast design. Third-party reviews consistently commend PebbleGo's emphasis on accessibility, noting its intuitive navigation, leveled texts, and multimodal supports that benefit a diverse range of learners, including those with disabilities and English language learners. The platform's recent Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT), which reflects an evaluation by both internal and third-party auditors, further validates its efforts. Designed to be mobile-responsive and optimized for various learning environments, PebbleGo aims to ensure equitable access for all K-3 students.
