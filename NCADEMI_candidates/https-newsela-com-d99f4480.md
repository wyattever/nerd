### AI Generated Insights
Newsela is committed to accessibility, designing its platform to meet WCAG 2.2 AA and Section 508 conformance standards. The company regularly conducts accessibility audits with third-party services and incorporates feedback from users into its continuous improvement process. Accessibility features include keyboard navigation, appropriate color contrast, alternative text for images, and support for screen readers. The platform provides specific accommodations for various learning needs, such as a read-aloud feature and closed captions for videos. Multiple states have recognized Newsela's commitment to accessibility, with its student experience being WCAG compliant to support students with disabilities.
