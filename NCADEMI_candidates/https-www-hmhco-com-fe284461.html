Vendor: Houghton Mifflin Harcourt

Houghton Mifflin Harcourt (HMH) is a K-12 learning company that provides integrated curriculum, assessment, and professional learning solutions to educators globally. They deliver digital-first EdTech products designed to foster growth and support diverse learning needs across various subjects like literacy, math, science, and social studies.
