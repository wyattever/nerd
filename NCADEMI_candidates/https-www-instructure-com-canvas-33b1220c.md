### AI Generated Insights
Canvas, a learning management system by Instructure, demonstrates a strong commitment to accessibility, adhering to WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and recently updating its Canvas Catalog to be fully compliant with WCAG 2.2 AA. The platform includes built-in accessibility features such as a Rich Content Editor accessibility checker and user-configurable settings for high contrast. Instructure regularly engages in third-party testing and considers accessibility a fundamental design principle, rather than a mere compliance checkbox. While the platform provides robust accessibility tools, several third-party resources emphasize that the ultimate accessibility of a course largely depends on content created by individual educators. Instructure offers various guidelines and resources to help instructors develop accessible course materials.
