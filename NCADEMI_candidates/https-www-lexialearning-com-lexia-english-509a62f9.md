# Lexia English
**Vendor:** Lexia Learning
**Description:** Lexia English Language Development™ is an adaptive blended learning program for K-6 Emergent Bilingual students designed to support English language acquisition through interactive, academic conversations. It integrates speaking, listening, and grammar across subjects such as math, science, social studies, general knowledge, and biographies. The program offers personalized learning paths with embedded progress monitoring and provides educators with actionable data and offline lessons.
**Product Website:** https://www.lexialearning.com/lexia-english

### AI Generated Insights
Lexia English is actively developed with a commitment to accessibility, incorporating continuous enhancements for keyboard operability, alternative text, captioning, and color contrast. The program aims to align with WCAG 2.0 and 2.1 A and AA criteria, with accessibility considerations integrated throughout its product lifecycle. A third-party review by Berkeley Public Schools, referencing a Lexia English VPAT, indicates some areas where the product partially supports or does not yet support certain WCAG standards. Identified deficiencies include aspects like name, role, value for interactive components, use of color, minimum contrast, and information and relationships. While progress is noted in areas like keyboard access and focus order, there are also limitations in areas such as resizing text without assistive technology and reflow.
