# BrainPOP
**Vendor:** BrainPOP
**Description:** BrainPOP is an educational technology platform offering animated movies, interactive quizzes, and activities across various subjects for students in grades K-8. It focuses on making complex topics engaging and accessible through multimedia content and supporting diverse learning styles.

### AI Generated Insights
BrainPOP demonstrates a strong commitment to accessibility, evidenced by its various features designed to support diverse learners, including closed captioning, adjustable playback speeds, movie transcripts, and multilingual support. The platform also integrates Microsoft Immersive Reader to aid students with reading challenges and offers embedded audio descriptions for visually impaired learners. While BrainPOP actively works to align with Universal Design for Learning principles, third-party assessments and its own VPAT indicate areas needing improvement, such as focus order and minimum contrast ratios in certain sections. The vendor continuously introduces new accessibility-focused features, highlighting an ongoing effort to enhance inclusivity across its offerings.

### AI Generated Insights
BrainPOP offers various accessibility features, including closed captions, full transcripts, and audio descriptions for movies, benefiting students who are deaf, hard of hearing, have low vision, or visual impairments. The platform supports Universal Design for Learning (UDL) by providing multiple means of representation, engagement, and expression, such as text, audio, video, and creative tools. Integrations like Microsoft Immersive Reader for quizzes enhance literacy support with tools like Line Focus, Text Personalization, and Read Aloud. Institutional audits and external reviews, including a WCAG-based VPAT, confirm BrainPOP's efforts to meet accessibility standards, though some areas for improvement in programmatic elements and keyboard navigation have been identified. Educational institutions also highlight BrainPOP's utility for differentiated instruction, supporting multilingual learners, and students with learning challenges by breaking down complex concepts and offering varied ways to demonstrate understanding.
