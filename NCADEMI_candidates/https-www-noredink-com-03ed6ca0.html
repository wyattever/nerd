NoRedInk

Vendor: NoRedInk

NoRedInk is an online writing and grammar curriculum platform for grades 3-12 that uses adaptive exercises and personalized content to help students improve their writing skills. It provides teachers with tools for instruction, practice, and assessment, including data-driven insights into student growth and features for academic integrity.

AI Generated Insights

NoRedInk demonstrates a significant commitment to accessibility, regularly updating its VPAT and detailing ongoing improvements that adhere to WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines. The platform undergoes third-party accessibility audits, reflecting a dedicated effort to enhance usability for all students and showing progress in reducing barriers. Substantial improvements have been made in areas like screen reader and keyboard compatibility for student-facing content. However, some identified issues persist, including challenges with certain tooltips, emojis lacking text alternatives, and low contrast text in specific elementary-grade content. The platform's continuous updates, including those detailed in its help center, further outline ongoing enhancements to navigation support. Its recognized benefits for students with dyslexia, adaptive learning capabilities, and features like text-to-speech highlight ongoing considerations for diverse user needs.
