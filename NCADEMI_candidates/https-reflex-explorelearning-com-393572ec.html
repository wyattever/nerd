Vendor: ExploreLearning

Reflex is an evidence-based, adaptive, and individualized EdTech solution designed to help students in grades 2 and up master basic math facts in addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division through engaging, game-based activities. It aims to build math confidence and fluency by providing personalized coaching and real-time progress monitoring.

AI Generated Insights

Reflex by ExploreLearning is designed to be accessible to all students, offering a highly visual, game-based math fact fluency platform with minimal text. It provides keyboard controls, closed captioning for voiced audio, optional audio, and contrasting colors. The platform supports personalized learning paths, special education students, and English language learners through features like Spanish and French language options and by factoring out keyboard speed from fluency calculations. However, it lacks adjustable font size or text zooming functionality. While the vendor provides articles detailing accessibility features, no specific VPAT or ACR document was found. Third-party accessibility reports are scarce, though an institutional audit from Berkeley Public Schools offers some external evaluation.
