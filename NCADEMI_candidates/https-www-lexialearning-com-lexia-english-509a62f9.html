Lexia English

Vendor: Lexia Learning

Lexia English Language Development™ is an adaptive blended learning program for K-6 Emergent Bilingual students designed to support English language acquisition through interactive, academic conversations. It integrates speaking, listening, and grammar across subjects such as math, science, social studies, general knowledge, and biographies. The program offers personalized learning paths with embedded progress monitoring and provides educators with actionable data and offline lessons.

AI Generated Insights

Lexia English is actively developed with a commitment to accessibility, continuously enhancing features like keyboard operability, alternative text, captioning, and color contrast. The program aims to align with WCAG 2.0 and 2.1 A and AA criteria, integrating accessibility throughout its product lifecycle. While progress is evident in areas such as keyboard access and focus order, institutional reviews highlight specific WCAG standards where the product partially supports or does not yet meet requirements. Identified deficiencies include aspects like name, role, value for interactive components, use of color, minimum contrast, information and relationships, and limitations in text resizing and reflow. A Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT 2.4Rev WCAG, January 2026) has been referenced, though a direct public link to the document is not readily available. Despite these ongoing areas for improvement, numerous school districts have approved Lexia English, often recognizing its contribution to addressing accessibility and equity for emergent bilingual students.
