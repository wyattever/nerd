# Sora
**Vendor:** OverDrive
**Description:** Sora is a free digital reading app developed by OverDrive for K-12 students. It provides access to ebooks, audiobooks, and magazines from participating school and public libraries, enabling students to read on their devices.
**Product Website:** https://soraapp.com/

### AI Generated Insights
Sora, by OverDrive, is designed with a strong focus on accessibility for K-12 students. Vendor resources highlight adherence to WCAG guidelines, offering extensive screen reader support for popular tools like ChromeVox, VoiceOver, NVDA, JAWS, and Android Accessibility Suite. The app includes features such as high contrast mode, dyslexic font, adjustable text sizes, and "Read to Me" text-to-speech functionality. Keyboard navigation and accessible page structure with alt text and skip links are also implemented to enhance usability. A Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) is available, detailing conformance with WCAG 2.0 and 2.1 Level A and AA standards.
