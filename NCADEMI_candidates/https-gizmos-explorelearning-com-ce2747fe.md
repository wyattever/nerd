### AI Generated Insights
Gizmos, developed by ExploreLearning, offers interactive STEM simulations for grades 3-12 with several accessibility features. The platform aims to be keyboard accessible for many simulations, using simple keystrokes for navigation, though some interactive elements may not be fully keyboard controllable. Content generally features minimal text with resizing capabilities via browser zoom, and sound effects can be toggled on or off. Most simulations do not rely on time-based responses, and color contrast is largely maintained across components, though some highly visual simulations may pose challenges for users with severe visual impairments. ExploreLearning continuously works to enhance the accessibility of its products.
