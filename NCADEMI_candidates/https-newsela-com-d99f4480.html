Newsela

Vendor: Newsela Inc.

Newsela is an educational technology platform that provides engaging, real-world content at differentiated reading levels to K-12 students. It aims to enhance literacy and learning outcomes across various subjects through a vast library of authentic texts, integrated assessments, and AI-powered tools.

AI Generated Insights

Newsela is deeply committed to accessibility, designing its educational platform to conform with WCAG 2.2 AA and Section 508 standards. The company regularly conducts third-party accessibility audits and integrates user feedback into its continuous improvement process, fostering an internal culture of accessibility. Key features include keyboard navigation, appropriate color contrast, alternative text for images, and robust support for screen readers. The platform also provides specific accommodations like a read-aloud feature and closed captions for videos to support diverse learning needs. Its accessibility posture is further documented through dedicated statements and conformance reports, and has been recognized by various states and third-party organizations for promoting equitable learning environments.
