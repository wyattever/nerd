# Book Creator
**Vendor:** Tools for Schools Limited
**Description:** Book Creator is an EdTech tool that enables students and teachers to create and publish interactive digital books with various multimedia elements. It is designed with Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles, aligning with WCAG 2.2 Level AA, EN 301 549, and Revised Section 508, to provide an accessible platform for all learners.
