Book Creator is an EdTech tool designed with Universal Design for Learning principles to provide an accessible platform for all learners. It aligns with key accessibility standards, including WCAG 2.2 Level AA, EN 301 549, and Revised Section 508. The platform offers detailed information on its accessibility features, standards conformance, and support for assistive technologies. Resources are available to guide users in creating accessible content within the tool, and a formal Accessibility Conformance Report is provided.
