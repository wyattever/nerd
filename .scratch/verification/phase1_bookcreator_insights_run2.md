Book Creator demonstrates a strong commitment to accessibility, aligning with WCAG 2.2 Level AA, EN 301 549, and Revised Section 508, as detailed in its Accessibility Conformance Report. The platform incorporates features such as screen reader support, keyboard navigation, dictation, and captions. Recent updates have improved contrast and overall usability for diverse learners. It also offers customizable fonts, backgrounds, and a 'Read to Me' function, making it particularly beneficial for students with visual impairments.
