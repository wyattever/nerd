Canvas is designed to facilitate accessible and inclusive online learning, with a commitment to regularly update and meet accessibility standards. The platform conforms with WCAG 2.2 Level A and AA, with specific components like Canvas Catalog meeting WCAG 2.2 AA. It integrates features such as an Accessibility Checker, High Contrast UI, and Immersive Reader to support diverse learning needs. Resources are provided for creating accessible courses, and a dedicated channel exists for reporting accessibility issues.
