# Book Creator
**Vendor:** Book Creator Ltd.
**Description:** Book Creator is an EdTech product that allows users to create and publish their own digital books. It is designed to empower students and educators to engage in learning and express their creativity through multimodal content creation. The platform works across various devices and browsers.
**Product Website:** https://bookcreator.com/
