Canvas is committed to providing an accessible and inclusive educational environment, adhering to Section 508, EN 301 549, and WCAG guidelines. Independent evaluations confirm its conformance with WCAG 2.2 Level A and AA, a standard also reflected in its refreshed VPATs. The platform offers various built-in accessibility features, including an Accessibility Checker, Rich Content Editor, Immersive Reader, keyboard shortcuts, and a High Contrast UI. Comprehensive resources and tools are available for instructors to create accessible content, and a direct channel exists for reporting accessibility issues.
