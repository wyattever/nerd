Canvas is designed to facilitate accessible and inclusive online learning, consistently updated to meet accessibility standards. The platform conforms with WCAG 2.2 Level A and AA, with specific components like Canvas Catalog meeting WCAG 2.2 AA. It integrates features such as an Accessibility Checker, High Contrast UI, and Immersive Reader to support diverse learning needs. External evaluations corroborate its adherence to these standards, and resources are available for creating accessible course content.
