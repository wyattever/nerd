### Research Rules
1. Grounding: You MUST enable and use "Grounding with Google Search" for every research query.
2. Relevance: Aggressively filter for accessibility-specific documentation (WCAG conformance, VPATs/ACRs, accessibility features, or independent audits).
3. Exclusions: Do not include general marketing, pricing, or "what is" pages that lack specific accessibility information.
4. Completeness: If a category (e.g., ground_truth_other) has no findings, return an empty list [].
