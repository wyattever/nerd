Epic

Vendor: Epic

Epic is a digital library and reading platform designed for children aged 12 and under, providing instant access to over 40,000 books, audiobooks, and educational videos. It aims to cultivate a love for reading and offers specific tools and resources for educators to use in classroom settings.

AI Generated Insights

Epic is committed to accessibility, offering built-in features such as speech-to-text search, Read-To-Me books with word highlighting, and audiobooks with adjustable playback speed. The platform provides specific resources for visual and hearing impairments, including a recent addition of dyslexic reader books. It integrates well with third-party assistive technologies like VoiceOver and TalkBack, enhancing its usability. Easy site navigation further contributes to its accessible design.
