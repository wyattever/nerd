Mastery Connect

Vendor: Instructure

Mastery Connect is a K–12 digital assessment management system that lets educators create and deliver both formative and benchmark assessments, tie results to specific academic standards, visualize student mastery through trackers and reports, and use that data to inform instructional decisions and interventions rather than just reporting overall scores.
